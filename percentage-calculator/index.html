Formula: X% of Y

Result = (X ÷ 100) × Y. For example, 25% of 200 = (25÷100)×200 = 50.

Formula: X of Y as %

Percentage = (X ÷ Y) × 100. For example, 50 is 25% of 200 because (50÷200)×100 = 25.

Percentage Change

Change% = ((New – Old) ÷ Old) × 100. A positive result is an increase; negative is a decrease.
