Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025 · We take your privacy seriously. Here's exactly what we do (and don't) collect.

🔒

The short version: Your files never leave your device.

iLovePDFs processes all files directly in your browser using WebAssembly. We never receive, store, or transmit your documents to any server. This is not a policy promise — it is technically impossible for us to see your files.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your PDF files or any documents you process
  • The content of your documents
  • Document metadata (author, creation date, etc.)
  • Passwords you enter for PDF protection/unlocking
  • Your signature data drawn in our signature tool

1.2 Information We May Collect

When you visit iLovePDFs, we may collect standard web analytics data including:

  • Usage analytics: Which tools are used most frequently, general usage patterns
  • Technical data: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution
  • Performance data: Page load times, error rates
  • Geographic data: Country-level location (not city or street-level)

All analytics data is anonymized and aggregated. We cannot identify individual users from this data.

2. How Your Browser Processes Files

Our tools use the following browser technologies to process files locally:

  • PDF-lib (WebAssembly): For creating, modifying, and saving PDFs
  • PDF.js: For rendering PDF pages and extracting text content
  • JSZip: For creating ZIP archives of multiple output files
  • HTML5 Canvas API: For image rendering and annotation drawing
  • File API: For reading files from your device without uploading

All processing happens in your browser's memory. When you close the tab or browser, all file data is discarded automatically. The "History" feature stores only file names and timestamps in your browser's local storage — not file content.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

We use minimal browser storage:

  • Theme preference: We store your light/dark mode preference in localStorage
  • Operation history: File names and timestamps of recent operations (no file content)
  • Session cookies: Standard browser session management (if applicable)

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics services that build user profiles.

4. Third-Party Services

iLovePDFs uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts: We load fonts from Google Fonts CDN. Google may collect standard CDN logs.
  • Cloudflare CDN (cdnjs): We load PDF processing libraries from cdnjs.cloudflare.com. Cloudflare may log standard access data.

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, Hotjar, or any other behavioral tracking services.

5. Contact and Support Communications

If you contact us through our contact form or email, we collect:

  • Your name and email address
  • The content of your message
  • The date and time of submission

We use this information solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not add you to marketing lists without explicit consent.

6. Children's Privacy

iLovePDFs is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Data Security

Since we don't collect your file data, there is no file data to secure or breach. For the minimal analytics and contact data we do collect:

  • All connections use HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Contact form submissions are stored encrypted
  • Access to any stored data is strictly limited

8. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights including:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@ilovepdfs.com. Since we collect very little personal data, most requests can be fulfilled immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes will be announced prominently on our website.

For privacy-related questions, please contact:
